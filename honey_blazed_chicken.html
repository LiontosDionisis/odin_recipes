Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

Fried chicken glazed with honey

Ingredients

  • 1.4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless,boneless chicken breast halves,cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk honey,soy sauce and red pepper flakes in a bowl and set it aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat,cook and stir chicken in hot oil untill it is lightly brown. (about 5 mins)
  4. Finally,pour honey mixture into the skillet and continue to cook and stir untill chicken is no longer pink in the center and the sauce is thickened. (about 5 more mins)
